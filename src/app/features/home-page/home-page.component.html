Why Should You Consider Donating?

Make a Life-Saving Impact

Each organ donation can save up to 8 lives and improve many others. Your decision to donate can give someone a second chance at life, reducing suffering and offering hope.

Donating is a Gift of Life

Organ donation is a simple act that creates profound change. By donating, you're joining a community of lifesavers and offering the gift of health to people in need.

You Can Help Reduce the Waiting List

Thousands of people are waiting for organ transplants. Your donation could make the difference and reduce the waiting time for many patients.

What Organs and Tissues Can You Donate?

You can donate various organs and tissues, either during your lifetime or after passing. Here are the organs you can donate:

Kidneys

Life-saving for patients with kidney failure.

Heart

Required by patients with heart failure.

Liver

Can regenerate, saving multiple lives.

Lungs

Helps patients with chronic lung diseases.

Pancreas

Helps people with severe diabetes.

Corneas

Restores vision for patients with blindness.
